# Eternal Mirage | Design System

## Vision
Branding for "Eternal Mirage" - A mystical desert retreat meets an executive digital tool. The aesthetic is Minimalist Digital Editorial, prioritizing aggressive whitespace, asymmetric layouts, and surgical digital clarity.

## Typography
- **Headings (Editorial):** `Alice` (Serif). Used for brand titles and primary section headers. Timeless and slightly calligraphic.
- **Body/UI (Precision):** `Noto Sans` (Sans-Serif). Used for all logistics, lists, and functional text. Clean and readable.
- **Formatting Rules:**
  - Headlines: ALL CAPS, font-serif.
  - Body Text: Standard capitalization, font-sans.
  - Data/Meta: Monospace (JetBrains Mono) for specific technical details.

## Component Specs
- **Cards & Sections:**
  - Border: 1px solid #E5E0D8.
  - Corners: 0px (Strictly Square).
  - Background: Transparent or #FFFFFF.
- **Dividers:** 
  - 1px solid horizontal lines (#E5E0D8) between major vertical content blocks.
- **Buttons / Links:**
  - Left-aligned, underlined, or framed with 1px border. No rounded corners.

## Spacing & Layout
- **Content Column:** Max-width 800px.
- **Editorial Gutters:** Large left/right margins on desktop to create a centered, gallery-like focus.
- **Alignment:** Asymmetric. Headlines left-aligned with padding; Body content centered in a narrow column or flush with margins.

## Motion
- **Entry:** Subtle 1s fade-ins for all sections.
- **Experimental:** "Mirage" effect (subtle blur + transform) for "Coming Soon" or mystical placeholder text.
